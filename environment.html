My environment in Armpac Compound, Banaba, San Mateo, Rizal is a small barangay. It is often muddy, especially when it rains. Even if it is not perfect, many people live here happily. The community is simple, but the neighbors are kind and helpful. You can see children playing and enjoying outside. Despite the challenges, it is a happy place to live. People help each other, especially in times of need. There are small stores where people can buy daily needs. Even if the roads are not always good, people learn to be patient. This place is full of happy memories with family and friends.
